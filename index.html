WHAT IS MANHATTAN COFFEE?

According to the SCA (Specialty Coffee Association) Sensory Evaluation Methodology, used worldwide, a specialty coffee is any coffee that scores at least 80 points on the scale (which goes up to 100). Manhattan Coffee stands out in fragrance, body, balance, sweetness, and flavor, featuring notes that may be fruity, herbal, or sweet like caramel and chocolate. We can compare it to wine, as Manhattan coffee can also be appreciated for its sensory characteristics and not just for its caffeine content. Thanks to excellent cultivation standards, post-harvest care, and roasting, you will be able to experience different and delicious types of coffee.

Life only begins after coffee!

DISCOVER MANHATTAN GOURMET COFFEE

The characteristics of Manhattan gourmet coffee are very distinctive. Its body depends on the blend variety: the heavier it feels in the mouth, the fuller the body. The lighter it feels, the lighter the body. Our gourmet coffee is highly rated throughout the production process to ensure it has no defects. The aroma is very noticeable, and customers can clearly perceive it. It may vary among four aroma profiles: floral, citrus, chocolatey, and fruity. These variations result from Arabica beans. Everything depends on the roast level — the lighter the roast, the higher the acidity.
